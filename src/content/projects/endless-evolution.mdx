## Overview

What started as a fun project with my crew is officially becoming our first commercial game.

Endless Evolution is a puzzle-platformer where you play as a slime, absorb animal DNA, gain new abilities, and use them to progress.

Watch the trailer on YouTube: [Endless Evolution Trailer](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gNwMo_rxAoc)

<YouTubeEmbed id="gNwMo_rxAoc" title="Endless Evolution Official Trailer" />

## The Origin

We originally built the game for JamSepticeye 2025. It was an intense 5-day marathon, and the results out of 1,158 entries gave us real momentum to keep going:

- Visuals: #18 (Thank you Jess!)
- Overall: #33

## What's New?

The jam version was just the beginning. We are now scaling the game, adding new mechanics, and expanding the world toward a full release.

The goal is to take that initial jam spark and turn it into a deeper, more polished experience.
